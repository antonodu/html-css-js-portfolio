About Me

My name is Anthony Odumodu. I'm a passionate and enthusiastic front-end developer based in London. I love coding and the limitless possibilities it offers. I hold a degree in Web Design and Development. I enjoy utilising web technologies to create seamless, user experiences. I enjoy working on passion projects that not only showcase my creativity but also challenge me to think outside the box. Embracing new challenges is something I relish, and I am always eager to learn and stay ahead of web development trends. You can explore my work in the project section. As a neurodivergent developer, I am committed to building inclusive and accessible digital spaces. I believe the web should be a place where everyone can engage meaningfully, and I use my unique perspective to make this vision a reality. I’m always open to discussing potential projects, collaborating on exciting ideas, or simply connecting. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me watching wrestling.
